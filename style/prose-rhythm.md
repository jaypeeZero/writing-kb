# Prose Rhythm

## Sentence Length Variation

- Mix short, punchy sentences with longer, flowing ones
- Short sentences create tension and emphasis
- Long sentences slow the pace and add detail or atmosphere
- A sudden short sentence after several long ones hits like a punch

## Paragraph Length

- Match paragraph length to pacing: short paragraphs for action, longer for reflection
- A one-sentence paragraph demands attention — use sparingly
- White space on the page is a pacing tool

## Sound and Flow

- Read your prose aloud — awkward rhythms become obvious
- Watch for accidental rhymes or repeated sounds
- Parallel structure creates a sense of momentum and inevitability
