# Dialogue

## Core Principles

- Dialogue should do at least two things at once: reveal character AND advance plot
- Cut greetings, small talk, and filler unless they serve characterization
- Each character should have a distinct voice pattern

## Common Pitfalls

- "Said bookisms" — prefer "said" and "asked" over "exclaimed", "retorted", etc.
- On-the-nose dialogue where characters say exactly what they feel
- Info-dumping through dialogue ("As you know, Bob...")
